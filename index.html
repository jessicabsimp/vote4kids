U.S. House

9 Districts Current: 8R · 1D

All nine Tennessee congressional seats are on the ballot. The most competitive race is TN-5 (Nashville-area), where the DCCC is targeting incumbent Andy Ogles. The TN-6 seat is open following John Rose's gubernatorial run, and TN-9 features a high-profile Democratic primary.

Knox County Mayor

R Primary May 5 · General Aug 6

Open seat — Mayor Glenn Jacobs (R) is term-limited. Three Republicans compete in the May 5 primary; Democrat Beau Hawk is unopposed and advances to the August 6 general. Knox County has not elected a Democratic mayor since 2002.
